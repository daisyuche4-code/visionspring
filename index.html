About Us

VisionSpring Initiative is a nonprofit organization founded in 2025 by Rejoice Samuel. We are committed to empowering individuals and transforming communities through sustainable development programs, education, leadership development, entrepreneurship training, and humanitarian support.


Vision

To build empowered, self-reliant and prosperous communities where every individual can reach their full potential.


Mission

To promote social development through education, empowerment, humanitarian interventions and sustainable community initiatives.
